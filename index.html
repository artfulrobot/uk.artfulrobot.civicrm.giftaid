CiviCRM Simple UK Gift Aid Extension

There's several Gift Aid extensions, what does this one do?

  1. It adds a field on every contribution to allow you to select whether it is eligible for Gift Aid, and whether Gift Aid has been claimed on it yet.

  2. It provides a Administer Gift Aid tool, launched from a Contributions search, that provides a spreadsheet in a format suitable for copy-and-pasting into an Inland Revenue claim spreadsheet from those matched contributions. It then marks those as claimed.

  3. In doing (2) it tries to use the rules around aggregate small donations to enable you to claim Gift Aid on contributions that lack of data (e.g. address) would have otherwise prevented you from claiming.

It does NOT:

  • automatically do anything. It's a tool to assist you in a fiddly task, not a robot.

  • do online submissions to HMRC.
